About Us

Bringing the Recovery Room to Your Living Room

Eagle River Recovery is a mobile wellness and recovery service operating in the Eagle River Valley region of Colorado, from Vail to Gypsum.

We deliver premium, in-home or condo-based recovery sessions and short-term equipment rentals directly to you. Whether you just crushed a day on the mountain or need to recover from training, we bring clinical-grade equipment right to your door.

No need to travel to a fixed studio or spa. Our team arrives with everything you need for a full recovery session, sets up in minutes, and guides you through the experience.
